"Connect with other students to share a ride to the University or to your hometown to reduce the

impact to the environment and save money"

EcoShare is a user controlled space where users connect to share rides, that way the user can

get to the destination whilst reducing the carbon footprint and saving money.

The aim of EcoShare is to provide a totally unregulated and open space where users can

facilitate “carpooling”. This is not a taxi service unlike other systems in place. Any user can set

up a ride and share to other users. All responsibility falls in the user, as with many other things.

By allowing these access to all users, EcoShare hopes to become the go-to solution for

students. Many students, and regular users, don’t own a vehicle and rely on others to get to

their locations. EcoShare hopes to open up a larger number of options for those who need it on

a daily basis, or those who need it from time to time, every user is welcome.
